SOUND AND SILENCE

|SOUND AND SILENCE| is a touring exhibition that will take place at three galleries across England in 2025. The exhibition brings together four sculptors and bell makers who explore the function and fabrication of bells in different ways. The exhibition will feature sonic sculptures, installations, performances, workshops and live bronze casting - shining a light on the history, making processes, contemporary position and sculptural nature of bells. Alongside the exhibition we will produce a radio documentary about the project.

Make Southwest, Devon

22 March â€“ 14 June 2025

This exhibition in MAKE Southwest's Jubilee Gallery and outdoor courtyard will show the history, making processes, contemporary position and sculptural nature of bells. It brings together 4 sculptors and bellmakers exploring the function and fabrication of bells - Marcus Vergette, sculptor, musician, and bellmaker; Copper Sounds, artist duo exploring 3D digital design and bronze casting; David Snoo Wilson, travelling founder and sculptor, and Emma-Kate Matthews, architect, composer, musician, and digital artist. The exhibition features sonic sculptures, installations, films, performances, workshops and live casting. It will examine and explain the history and impact, past, present and future, of bell making and bell ringing in an exhibition that will be informative, fun and accessible for all ages, and feature plenty of events and chances to make some noise!
